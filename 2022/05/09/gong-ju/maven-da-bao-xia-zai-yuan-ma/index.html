​ 使用Maven时，有时下载jar需要源码， 需要加以下Maven插件

<plugin>
	<groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
	<artifactId>maven-source-plugin</artifactId>
	<version>3.0.0</version>
	<configuration>
		<attach>true</attach>
	</configuration>
	<executions>
		<execution>
			<phase>compile</phase>
			<goals>
				<goal>jar</goal>
			</goals>
		</execution>
	</executions>
</plugin>

​ 另外,可通过以下命令, 手动下载源码

mvn dependency:sources
mvn dependency:resolve -Dclassifier=javadoc
